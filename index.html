Hello there! Apa khabar?

My name is Michael and I love meeting people from all corners of the world and showcasing the wonders of KL and beyond. I enjoy it so much that I've been doing it for the past 20 years.

Trips with me are not only fun, they're also filled with information and interesting facts. For a truly immersive experience, I find it important to provide my guests with historical backgrounds and intertwine fascinating stories about places we visit and things we come across during our adventures.

I look forward to leaving you with an unforgettable experience.
