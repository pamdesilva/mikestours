City and Country Tour

Embark on scenic drive and visit the most popular sights in Kuala Lumpur including a stop at magnificent limestone caves that are over 400 million years old with a grand temple to match. Watch a first-hand demonstration of the traditional art of batik painting and get an insight look into how rubber is milked from trees.

Attractions to be explored during the tour:

  • Old Kuala Lumpur Sultan Abdul Samad building – the first government administrative centre
  • Batu Caves temple
  • Rubber Tapping , Batik Painting and Pewter- making demonstration
  • King’s New Palace and the National Monument

    • Price per pax: RM100 (min 2 pax)

    Tour duration: Approximately 4 hours

    What’s included: Pick-up and drop-off from hotel.

    What to bring/wear: Casual clothing, comfortable walking shoes, water, sunglasses, and hats.

    Melawati Hills and Fireflies Tour

    A popular tour among animal lovers, this tour will allow you to get up close and personal with the adorable Silverleaf monkeys while taking in the stunning views from the top of Melawati Hill. The evening is followed by a scrumptious dinner in a local fishing village overlooking the Selangor River before winding down with a tranquil boat ride while being mesmerized by hundreds of thousands of glowing fireflies.

    Attractions to be explored during the tour:

  • Silverleaf Monkeys sighting and feeding
  • Evening view from the top of Melawati Hill
  • Sunset dinner at local fishing village
  • Fireflies sighting along Selangor River

    • Price per pax: RM280 (Min 2 pax)

    Tour duration: Approximately 6 hours

    What’s included: Pick-up and drop-off from hotel, dinner and fireflies entrance ticket

    What to bring/wear:Casual clothing, comfortable walking shoes, water, sunglasses, hats, and mosquito repellent.

    Melaka Day Trip

    Approximately a 2 hour drive from Kuala Lumpur, the historic state of Melaka is a fascinating combination of remnants of Malaysian history, multicultural heritage, beautiful architecture, and delicious flavours courtesy of the Baba Nyonya culture and cuisine. On this tour, you will get to visit popular local sights with visible Dutch influences including forts, churches, and historic residences while sampling delicious local cuisine.

    Attractions to be explored during the tour:

  • Jonker Street
  • A Famosa Fort
  • Christ Church Melaka
  • Old Dutch Administrative Centre

    • Price per pax: RM350 (Min 2 pax)

    Tour duration: Approximately 7 hours

    What’s included: Pick-up and drop-off from hotel and light local lunch.

    What to bring/wear: Casual clothing, comfortable walking shoes, water, sunglasses, and hats.

    KL Evening Cultural Tour

    Experience the colourful sights and sounds of Kuala Lumpur by night. Revel in the architecture of oldest Hindu temple in KL and walk through the hustle and bustle of Chinatown where you can sample various street foods and shop at the many stalls selling intricate finds among a sea of merchandise. End the evening with a traditional cultural performance of song and dance, while you tuck into a sumptuous Malay dinner.

    Attractions to be explored during the tour:

  • Chinatown
  • Maha Mariamman Temple
  • Malay Buffet Dinner with Cultural Show

    • Price per pax: RM230 (Min 2 pax)

    Tour duration: Approximately 6 hours

    What’s included: Pick-up and drop-off from hotel and dinner.

    What to bring/wear: Casual clothing, comfortable walking shoes, and water.

    Cameron Highlands Day Trip

    If you’re in need to cool off from the KL heat and crave a little tranquillity, then a day trip to Cameron Highlands just may be what you need. An approximate 3 hour drive from KL, see the gorgeous sights of mountains and hills as you’re driven up to Cameron Highlands. On the way, you will stop at the largest waterfall Cameron Highlands has to offer and even watch a fascinating demonstration of blow pipes by aboriginal folk. The day continues with visits to picture-worthy farms and a stunning tea plantation.

    Attractions to be explored during the tour:

  • Demonstration of Blow Pipe by the aboriginal tribal people of Malaysia
  • Lata Iskandar waterfall
  • Strawberry farm
  • Flower nursery
  • Tea Plantation

    • Price per pax: RM350 (Min 2 pax)

    Tour duration: Approximately 7 hours

    What’s included: Pick-up and drop-off from hotel.

    What to bring/wear: Casual clothing, comfortable walking shoes, cardigan or jumper, bathing suit, water, sunglasses, and hats.

    Genting Highlands Day Trip

    An alternative option for a cooling escape from the KL heat is a day trip to the Malaysia’s mountain top complete with a theme park and casino. To get there, you will journey on a 45 minute drive from KL with scenic sights to capture along the way. Then embark on a 30 minute cable car ride to the mountain top where all the fun wonders of Genting Highlands await you.

    Attractions to be explored during the tour:

  • Scenic drive from KL to Cable Car Station at Gohtong Jaya
  • Genting Casino*
  • Genting Theme Park for Adults and Children**

    Price per pax: RM280 (Min 2 pax)

    Tour duration: Approximately 7 hours

    What’s included: Pick-up and drop-off from hotel and cable car ticket.

    What to bring/wear: Casual clothing, comfortable walking shoes, cardigan or jumper, water, sunglasses, and hats.

    The Crab Island Tour

    The Crab Island tour will allow you to witness the daily life of local fishermen in an authentic fishing village built on stilts. For this tour, you will be driven out of KL to the west of Selangor, passing through the royal town of Shah Alam where you will stopover at the largest mosque in Malaysia. Then, you will hop on a ferry to Crab Island where you will get to see local fishermen in action and get a glimpse of the life and community in a fishing village.

    Attractions to be explored during the tour:

  • Drive through Shah Alam
  • Visit Sultan Salahuddin Abdul Aziz Mosque (Blue Mosque)
  • Ferry ride to Crab Island
  • Fishing village

    • Price per pax: RM210 (Min 2 pax)

    Tour duration: Approximately 7 hours

    What’s included: Pick-up and drop-off from hotel and lunch.

    What to bring/wear: : Casual clothing, comfortable walking shoes, water, sunglasses, and hats.
